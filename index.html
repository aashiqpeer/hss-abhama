About PM SHRI School

PM SHRI Govt. Higher Secondary School Abhama is a proud institution under the centrally sponsored scheme – PM SHRI Schools (PM ScHools for Rising India). We aim to provide holistic, inclusive, and quality education with a modern curriculum rooted in Indian values.

Our campus is equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure, smart classrooms, vocational labs, and trained faculty. We nurture students to become responsible citizens and future leaders.

📌 Affiliation: JKBOSE | Medium: English, Urdu | Classes: 6th to 12th

Our Mission

To empower every child with knowledge, skills, and values through an experiential learning environment under the PM SHRI framework.

Our Vision

To be a model school of excellence that transforms rural education into global opportunities.
